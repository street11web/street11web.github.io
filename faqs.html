Street 11 FAQs

How to create a Street 11 account?

Install Street 11 app from Google Play Store. Open the app. Sign in using Google. A Street 11 account will be created automatically, when you sign in for the first time.

What is my username and password?

Street 11 currently supports only signing in using Google. So, Street 11 accounts do not have a separate username and password. Accounts are identified using email addresses provided while signing in using Google.

How to sign out of my account?

Click on top left corner in the app home page. Click on 'My Account' . Click on 'Sign Out' button.

How to delete my Street 11 account?

Sign in to your account in the mobile app. Click on top left corner in the app home page. Click on 'My Account'. Click on 'Delete Account' at the bottom of the screen. Type 'delete' in the text h3. Click 'Confirm'.

What happens to my page after my account is deleted?

Your page might remain active for 1 day after your account is deleted. After that, your page will be deleted and the address will be available for others to use.

Can I re-activate my account after deleting it?

No. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be re-activated. You will have to create a new account with a different email address. The same email can't be used for creating a new account.
